About Me

Hello! I'm Kunal, a freelance video editor with four years of dedicated experience bringing stories to life. I've had the privilege of working with over 3 digital marketing companies and more than 40 individual clients, delivering high-quality video content that meets their strategic goals. My journey in post-production has equipped me with a versatile skill set, allowing me to excel in creating everything from snappy, attention-grabbing short-form videos to comprehensive, narrative-driven long-form content.

I am highly proficient with the industry-standard Adobe Creative Suite, including Premiere Pro and After Effects, as well as DaVinci Resolve for advanced color grading and finishing. This technical expertise allows me to focus on what truly matters: the story. I believe that every cut, transition, and sound cue should serve the narrative, creating a seamless and immersive experience for the viewer.

If you're looking for a reliable, creative, and detail-obsessed editor to elevate your next project, let's connect.
